Chakra Loop Privacy Policy

Chakra Loop is a personal productivity and self-tracking app.

Data

The app stores user-created routines, tasks, course progress, habit logs, notes, and related personal tracking data.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud Sync is enabled, user-created data may be synced between the user's own Apple devices using Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services.

Tracking

Chakra Loop does not use third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics.

Accounts

No separate account is required to use the app.
